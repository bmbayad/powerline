Our Work!

TPV is responsible for validating the thermal and power properties and specifications of Intel products. This involves system to tester correlation of metrics such as leakage, Cdyn, and TDP, as well as debug and tuning of thermal and power related features. TPV tests are run on a wide range of material in order conduct validation with a focus on manufacturing variability.
